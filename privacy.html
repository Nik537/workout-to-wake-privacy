Workout to Wake — Privacy Policy

Effective: July 13, 2026

Workout to Wake does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

  • No accounts. The app works entirely on your device.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no ads. The app contains no third-party SDKs.
  • Camera processing stays on your device. The front camera and Apple's Vision framework identify body-joint positions to count push-ups and squats. Frames, images, and joint data are processed in real time and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • No microphone access. The app does not request or use the microphone.
  • Purchases are processed by Apple. We never see your payment details. Subscription status is verified on-device via Apple's StoreKit.
  • Alarms are stored locally on your device only.

Because we collect nothing, there is nothing on our servers to retain, sell, share, or delete. You can delete locally stored alarms in the app and remove all local app data by deleting the app.

You can withdraw camera or alarm permission at any time in iPhone Settings. The related camera-counting or alarm feature will stop working until permission is restored.

If you have questions, contact: optinutrinerds@gmail.com. For troubleshooting, visit Workout to Wake Support.
