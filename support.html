Workout to Wake — Support

Need help with an alarm, camera mission, or subscription? Email optinutrinerds@gmail.com.

Alarm does not ring

Open iPhone Settings and confirm that Workout to Wake is allowed to schedule alarms. Keep the alarm enabled in the app.

Exercise is not counted

Allow camera access, place the whole body in frame, use a well-lit room, and complete each repetition through its full range of motion. Camera processing stays on your iPhone.

Subscription help

Use Restore Purchases on the paywall. You can manage or cancel a subscription in iPhone Settings under Apple Account → Subscriptions.
